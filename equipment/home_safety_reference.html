How to use this reference page

Use the equipment categories as a practical checklist after a home safety screen. The best first changes are usually the ones that reduce fall risk in the highest-use areas.

Start with high-risk areas

Bathrooms, entrances, bedroom-to-bathroom paths, and stairs often deserve early attention because a small hazard can have a large impact.

Match equipment to the problem

Use grab bars for transfer support, lighting for visibility, cord covers for trip hazards, and reachable storage solutions for overreaching or climbing.

Confirm fit and installation

Some items require proper mounting, sizing, or placement. When safety is uncertain, involve a qualified professional or caregiver before relying on the equipment.
