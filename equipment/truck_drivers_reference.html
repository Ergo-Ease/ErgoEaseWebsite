How to use this page

The goal is not to buy everything. It is to identify the changes most likely to improve comfort, reduce strain, and support a better fit between the driver and the cab.

Start with the highest-friction task

Choose the category that best matches the part of driving that feels hardest right now, such as low-back discomfort, mirror checks, glare, or cab entry.

Prioritize high-impact fixes

Seat support, vibration control, pedal reach, and steering comfort usually create the biggest day-to-day improvements for truck drivers.
