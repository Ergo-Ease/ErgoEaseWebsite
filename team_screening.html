Simple team workflow

Team screening follows the same logic every time — the only thing that changes is the specific employee screen you share.

01

Choose a screen type

Select the role or environment that best fits the team you want to screen.

02

Complete employer setup

Use the employer form to configure the team workflow and reporting threshold.

03

Share the employee screen

Send the correct occupation-specific or home safety screen to the people completing it.

04

Receive reporting automatically

Individual reports are created per response, and the employer summary is sent after the response requirement is met.
